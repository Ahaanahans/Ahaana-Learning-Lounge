About me

The short version: I like numbers that behave and routines that don't.

Dear reader,

If you ask my teachers, I'm the kid who finishes the maths worksheet early and then asks for a harder one. If you ask my dance instructor, I'm the one who still wants to redo the eight-count nobody noticed was off. Both are true, and I've stopped trying to pick one over the other.

Mathematics, Science, Public Speaking, Writing & Storytelling, and AI are the five subjects I keep coming back to — each one builds a different muscle. Maths and Science sharpen my analytical thinking. Public Speaking has made me far more confident than I used to be. Writing & Storytelling let me put ideas into words on my own terms. And AI is where I get to chase real-world problems with tools that didn't exist a few years ago.

I've represented my school in debates, quizzes, speeches, declamation, character enactment, GDG MUN, and event hosting — basically, if there's a stage, a mic, or a motion to be debated, I'm probably signing up.

Currently studying for: a country that needs better civil servants.
